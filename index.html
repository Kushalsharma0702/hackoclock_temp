About HackOClock

HackOClock is a 24-hour coding marathon where innovators, developers, and creators come together to build cutting-edge solutions. Join us for an unforgettable experience of coding, learning, and networking.

Our mission is to provide a platform for enthusiastic developers to collaborate, innovate, and transform ideas into reality.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is HackOClock?

+

HackOClock is a 24-hour hackathon where participants collaborate to build innovative solutions to real-world problems.

Who can participate?

+

Anyone with a passion for coding and innovation can participate. Students, professionals, and enthusiasts are all welcome!

Is there a registration fee?

+

No, participation in HackOClock is completely free of charge.

What should I bring?

+

Bring your laptop, charger, and any hardware you might need for your project. Don't forget your ID for check-in!

Will there be food?

+

Yes, we'll provide meals, snacks, and beverages throughout the event to keep you energized and focused.

How are the winners selected?

+

Projects will be judged based on innovation, technical complexity, user experience, and presentation by a panel of industry experts.
